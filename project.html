Baking

My way of relaxing

Baking is the process of cooking by dry heat, especially in some kind of oven. It is probably the oldest cooking method. Bakery products, which include bread, rolls, cookies, pies, pastries, and muffins, are usually prepared from flour or meal derived from some form of grain. Baking is a great hobby that help you take a mental break from everyday life. Creating treats in the kitchen does not have to be done alone, they are perfect for spending pleasant evenings together and enjoying the homemade delicacies with company.

If you are looking for some inspiration, you can find it at Sally`s baking addiction, one of my favorite blogs.
